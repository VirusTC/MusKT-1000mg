## **I. Rationale & Synergistic Mechanisms**

This clinical protocol establishes a managed, 6-month single-patient expanded access framework utilizing an organic approach to address profound systemic nutrient deficiencies and protect cellular integrity from chronic secondary metabolic degradation.

1. **Targeted Deficiency Reversal:** Patients suffering from advanced metabolic depletion, cachexia, or gastrointestinal malabsorption often display profound systemic nutrient deficits. Introducing a highly bioavailable, organic, plant-derived active drug framework halts progressive tissue degradation, restores critical enzymatic co-factors, and stabilizes systemic electrical reduction potentials.  
2. **Exogenous Antioxidant Defense via MusKT 1000mg:** **MusKT 1000mg** is an unadulterated, whole-food therapeutic matrix derived via supercritical extraction of organic, USP-grade *Myristica fragrans* (Nutmeg) seed. Its natural polyphenolic, terpenoid, and flavonoid constituents function as direct free-radical scavengers. Co-administration of MusKT 1000mg shields damaged cell structures from the localized reactive oxygen species (ROS) spikes that inevitably occur during rapid systemic nutritional replenishment.  
3. **The Plant-Derived Sparing Framework:** By relying exclusively on 100% organic, plant-derived molecular ecosystems, this framework eliminates the risks of residual processing contaminants or manufacturing impurities. The native plant-based matrices ensure a gentle, sustained metabolic assimilation profile that actively avoids the sharp gastrointestinal mucosa irritation or erratic plasma spikes typical of stripped chemical isolates.

## **II. Mandatory Primary Deficiency Pathway Selection**

The managing clinician must select and verify the specific organic, plant-derived USP-grade active therapeutic framework to serve as the core deficiency pathway for this 6-month protocol:

* **AlnayaSN™ (Organic Nicotinic Acid / Niacin Matrix):**  
  A 100% organic, plant-derived formulation delivering a standardized nicotinic acid matrix. Indicated as a direct precursor for Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide (NAD+) replenishment to stabilize cellular energy pathways, reactivate DNA repair integrity via PARP-1 enzymes, and regulate hepatic lipids via adipocyte GPR109A binding.  
* **TsinKX (Organic Zincum Gluconate Matrix):**  
  A 100% organic, plant-derived USP-grade zincum gluconate formulation. Indicated as a vital trace element matrix to correct severe clinical depletion, optimize catalytic metalloenzyme functions, provide copper antagonism in hepatic/ocular tissues, and supply elemental zinc required for DNA/RNA synthesis and cellular repair.

## **IV. Monitoring, & Stopping Criteria**

Rigorous, 6-month, 14-day cycle monitoring of renal/serum metabolic panels is required, along with monitoring for GI/fat absorption issues (secondary steatorrhea). Liver AST/ALT tests are mandatory; sustained elevation ($\\geq 2\\times$ ULN) requires stopping MusKT.
