## SECTION 2: THE DIETARY SUPPLEMENT ROOT STRUCTURE
*This framework applies to formulations classified under retail or commercial supplement pathways. It strictly targets the support of normal, healthy human biological structures and functions.*

### The Core Legal Root
**Structural and Functional Homeostasis Support:** This product is structured to support the body’s natural immune barriers, promote gastrointestinal comfort, and maintain healthy cellular lifecycles already within a normal range.

### Permissible Labeling Claims (Consumer Packaging)
Every supporting claim on consumer packaging must connect directly back to the maintenance of normal structure or function:

* **Cellular Lifecycle Root:** **"Supports healthy cellular lifecycles and natural programmed cell death (apoptosis) mechanisms."**
* **Barrier Integrity Root:** **"Helps maintain the structural integrity of the body’s natural immune and mucosal barriers."**
* **Digestive Function Root:** **"Promotes gastrointestinal comfort and supports normal motility within the digestive tract."**
* **Antioxidant Defense Root:** **"Provides robust antioxidant support to protect healthy cells and tissues from oxidative damage."**

## SECTION 3: THE MANDATORY FEDERAL COMPLIANCE FOOTER

### For the Supplement Pathway Only
Any commercial packaging utilizing the Dietary Supplement Root Structure must display the standard federal disclaimer exactly as written:
> **"This statement has not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."**

### For the Medical Food Pathway Only
Any formulation utilizing the Medical Food Root Structure must omit the supplement disclaimer and replace it with the mandatory professional restriction:
> **"Directions for Use: For use under the direct and continuous supervision of a licensed physician or medical professional only."**
