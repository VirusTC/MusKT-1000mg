## **I. Rationale & Synergistic Mechanisms**

This protocol establishes a managed, 6-month single-patient expanded access framework utilizing an organic, dual-pathway approach to correct severe metabolic imbalances and manage chronic crystallization risk.

## **I. Rationale & Synergistic Mechanisms**

The protocol utilizes a 6-month, dual-pathway approach for metabolic management and crystallization risk:

1. **Systemic & Urinary Alkalization:** An organic citrate matrix creates a steady-state alkaline load, shifting urinary pH to a 8.0 target to reduce crystal formation.  
2. **Exogenous Antioxidant Support:** **MusKT** (standardized *Myristica fragrans* seed extract) provides polyphenolic, terpenoid, and flavonoid constituents to shield against oxidative stress, supporting the protocol requirements.  
3. **Plant-Derived Sparing Framework:** Utilizing natural citrate matrices avoids mineral precipitation, with bioflavonoids (hesperidin/eriocitrin) and dimeric phenylpropanoids offering a gentle, sustained metabolic approach.

## **III. Standardized Clinical Protocol & Administration**

* **MusKT** is taken daily with the morning citrus dose.  
* **Citrus Protocols:** Taken 4 times daily, every 6 hours.  
* **Dental Care:** A mandatory 5-minute post-dose water rinse is required.  
* **Safety Exception:** If the patient presents with WHO Visual Mucositis Grade $\\geq 2$, the protocol must be paused.

## **IV. Safety Monitoring & Mandatory Stopping Criteria**

* **Metabolic Panels:** Serum electrolytes ($Na^+$, $K^+$, $Cl^-$, $HCO\_3^-$) are monitored bi-weekly. If $HCO\_3^-$ exceeds 32 mEq/L, reduce the dose.  
* **Urinary Monitoring:** Monthly urinalysis is required. If calcium phosphate crystals form, adjust pH target to 7.5.  
* **Hepatic Monitoring:** Monthly AST/ALT monitoring is required. If liver enzymes exceed $\\geq 2\\times$ the ULN, discontinue MusKT.
