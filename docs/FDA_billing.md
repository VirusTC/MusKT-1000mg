Clinical Documentation: Monitoring & Billing Matrix
---------------------------------------------------

1\. Evaluation & Management (E&M) Code Selector Table

When a physician oversees a patient's medical food or specialized botanical protocol within a closed hospital or clinic system, they must utilize standard E&M codes. The level of billing is determined strictly by the complexity of the case or total time spent on the date of the encounter. [[1](https://www.ama-assn.org/system/files/2023-e-m-descriptors-guidelines.pdf), [2](https://neolytix.com/articles/e-m-changes/), [3](https://www.ama-assn.org/system/files/regulatory-myths-doc-coding-em.pdf), [4](https://www.rapidclaims.ai/blogs/navigating-the-2026-evaluation-and-management-e-m-coding-guidelines-key-highlights)]

| CPT Code | Patient Status | Setting | Code Level | Required Total Time (2026 Guidelines) | Medical Decision Making (MDM) Level |
| **99203** | New Patient | Office / Outpatient | Level 3 | **30--44 minutes** | Low Complexity |
| **99204** | New Patient | Office / Outpatient | Level 4 | **45--59 minutes** | Moderate Complexity |
| **99205** | New Patient | Office / Outpatient | Level 5 | **60--74 minutes** | High Complexity |
| **99213** | Established Patient | Office / Outpatient | Level 3 | **20--29 minutes** | Low Complexity |
| **99214** | Established Patient | Office / Outpatient | Level 4 | **30--39 minutes** | Moderate Complexity |
| **99215** | Established Patient | Office / Outpatient | Level 5 | **40--54 minutes** | High Complexity |
| **99232** | Subsequent Care | Hospital Inpatient / Obs. | Level 2 | **35--49 minutes** | Moderate Complexity |
| **99233** | Subsequent Care | Hospital Inpatient / Obs. | Level 3 | **50--64 minutes** | High Complexity |

Prolonged Services Add-on Code

-   **CPT 99417**: Use this add-on code for each additional **15 minutes** spent with the patient beyond the maximum time limit of a Level 5 office visit (`99205` or `99215`). [[1](https://webfiles.gi.org/links/pm/KuoSlides.pdf)]

2\. Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT) Specific Codes

If the clinical monitoring is carried out strictly for nutritional management without an expanded physical examination, the following targeted, time-based codes apply: [[1](https://www.findacode.com/articles/billing-nutrition-counseling-33806.html)]

```
           [ 15-Minute Base Units ]
                      │
     ┌────────────────┴────────────────┐
     ▼                                 ▼
 [ 97802: Initial Visit ]    [ 97803: Follow-Up Visit ]
     │                                 │
     └─► Comprehensive Intake          └─► Protocol Adjustments
     └─► Baseline Labs Reviewed        └─► Biomarker Trend Auditing

```

-   **CPT 97802**: Medical nutrition therapy; **initial assessment** and intervention, individual, face-to-face, **each 15 minutes**.
-   **CPT 97803**: Medical nutrition therapy; **re-assessment** and intervention, individual, face-to-face, **each 15 minutes**. [[1](https://reimbursementdietitian.com/cpt-codes-dietitians-can-use-to-bill/), [2](https://pabau.com/procedure-codes/cpt-code-97804/)]

3\. Objective Data Tracking Chart (Physician Lab Flow)

To justify the clinical necessity of billing high-level E&M codes, a doctor's chart must show objective tracking of patient biomarkers. Below is the standard protocol for tracing target biochemical root systems: [[1](https://www.cms.gov/training-education/medicare-learning-networkr-mln/compliance/medicare-provider-compliance-tips/evaluation-management-services), [2](https://www.ama-assn.org/system/files/regulatory-myths-doc-coding-em.pdf)]

```
[Patient Lab Order] ──► [Eugenol / Myristicin Input] ──► [Serum/Tissue Audit] ──► [CPT Documentation]

```

| Monitored Pathway | Core Biological Target | Objective Lab Biomarker to Track | Clinical Monitoring Goal |
| **Inflammatory Root** | COX-2, TNF-α, IL-6 | - High-Sensitivity CRP\
- Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate (ESR)\
- Plasma IL-6 levels | Audit systematic reduction of localized tissue stress. |
| **Oxidative Root** | Lipid Peroxidation | - Oxidized LDL\
- Serum Malondialdehyde (MDA)\
- Total Antioxidant Capacity (TAC) | Track protection of healthy cell membranes. |
| **Metabolic/Hepatic Root** | Liver Clearances | - AST / ALT liver enzymes\
- GGT\
- Total Bilirubin | Monitor organ safety and metabolic processing thresholds. |
| **Cellular Lifecycle** | Apoptotic Turnover | - Circulating Cell-Free DNA (cfDNA)\
- Serum Caspase-3 markers | Document safe cellular turnover rates. |
