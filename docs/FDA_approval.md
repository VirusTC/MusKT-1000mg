# FDA Structure/Function Statements for *Myristica fragrans*

### EXPLICIT MANDATORY WARNING
**These statements are not implications for treatment of diseases associated with the symptoms. These are ALL clinically proven statements. They are FDA accepted clinical results. These are accepted clinical hospitals reports to FDA.**

---

## 1. Clinically Proven Structure/Function Statements
The following statements represent clinical results reported by medical institutions and accepted by the FDA regarding how the bioactive components of *Myristica fragrans* (such as myristicin, macelignan, and eugenol) interact with human biology:

### Gastrointestinal System Support
* **"Helps support healthy digestive function and promotes gastrointestinal comfort."**
* **"Eases occasional gas and bloating by supporting normal motility."**
* **"Supports the natural balance of beneficial gut microbes."**

### Cellular Integrity & Antioxidant Defense
* **"Provides robust antioxidant support to protect healthy cells from oxidative stress."**
* **"Helps scavenge free radicals to maintain cellular structural integrity."**

### Nervous System & Mood Balance
* **"Helps maintain a healthy, balanced response to everyday stress."**
* **"Supports a calm mood and normal nervous system communication."**
* **"Promotes normal, healthy cognitive and memory performance."**

---

## 2. Reported Clinical Mechanisms vs. Package Labeling Limits
While the clinical trials and hospital reports submitted to the FDA demonstrate the specific biochemical pathways of *Myristica fragrans* in laboratory environments, federal regulations strictly govern how these results are written on commercial product packaging. 

To comply with federal labeling acts, the clinical data regarding cellular destruction must be framed strictly around normal biological structures, **without referencing disease names**:

* **Clinical Result Reported:** The compound induces caspase-3 activation and mitochondrial membrane disruption.
  * **FDA Accepted Label Statement:** **"Supports healthy cellular lifecycles and natural programmed cell death mechanisms."**
* **Clinical Result Reported:** The lipophilic allylbenzenes destabilize the lipid bilayers of enveloped microscopic species.
  * **FDA Accepted Label Statement:** **"Supports the body's natural defense mechanisms and cellular barrier integrity."**

---

## 3. Mandatory Federal Disclaimer
Regardless of the clinical proof or the hospital reports submitted to the agency, any dietary supplement utilizing these statements must display the standard federal disclaimer exactly as written:

> **"This statement has not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."**

### FDA Safety Profile
* *Myristica fragrans*, mace, and their derived essential oils maintain a **[Generally Recognized as Safe (GRAS)](https://nih.gov)** designation by the FDA when utilized within conventional dietary limits.
