# Corporate Regulatory Profile & Clinical Labeling Framework
**Product Classification:** Medical Food  
**Distribution Channel:** Controlled Clinical Distribution (Hospitals, Specialized Clinics, and Licensed Physicians Only)  
**Exemption Status:** Exempt from DSHEA Supplement Disclaimer (21 U.S.C. 343(r)(6)) and Standard Nutrition Facts Labeling (21 CFR 101.9(j)(8))

---

## 1. Statutory Authority & Disclaimer Exemption
Because this formulation is classified as a Medical Food as defined by section 5(b) of the Orphan Drug Act (21 U.S.C. 360ee(b)(3)), it is explicitly intended for the dietary management of a patient who has special medically evaluated nutritional requirements. 

Consequently, **the product does not carry, and is exempt from, the standard retail dietary supplement disclaimer** ("This statement has not been evaluated..."). It is instead governed by direct clinical oversight where a licensed physician has already established the patient's diagnostic framework.

---

## 2. Mandatory Institutional Labeling Requirements
To maintain its standing as a medical food and prevent misbranding under FDA compliance guidelines, the product container and clinical insert must feature specific visual markers and text blocks:

### Required Front-Panel Statement
The label must prominently state the following restriction to reinforce its clinical nature:
> **"Directions for Use: For use under the direct and continuous supervision of a licensed physician or medical professional only."**

### Distinctive Dietary Management Statement
In place of general wellness or structure/function claims, the labeling must detail the specific metabolic or physiological requirements it addresses. For example:
> **"This product is a specially formulated medical food intended for the distinctive dietary management of metabolic imbalances, localized inflammatory tissue stress, and nutrient absorption deficits associated with clinically diagnosed cellular and immune system disorders."**

## 4. Hospital Formulary & Closed-System Distribution Protocols
To preserve the legal status of this formulation as a medical food and prevent any accidental intersection with over-the-counter (OTC) retail regulations, the following distribution barriers are maintained:
1. **No Public Commercialization:** The product is completely restricted from standard retail settings, online consumer marketplaces, and general public access.
2. **Hospital Formulary Integration:** Dispensing is managed through institutional inpatient pharmacies, outpatient specialty clinics, or directly via a licensed physician’s clinical dispensary.
3. **Professional In-Service Guidance:** Technical dossiers detailing the exact molecular extraction profiles, batch purity validation, and clinical rationale are distributed exclusively to medical directors, oncology/pathology departments, and hospital pharmacy boards.
